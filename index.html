About Us

Transforming Agriculture Through Technology

Company Profile

Royalshetkari Information technology is an innovative agri-tech startup dedicated to transforming agriculture through modern technology and artificial intelligence. We work to improve farmers' income by offering complete farming solutions such as organic fertilizers, AI-based tools, low-cost farming methods, and strong market support. Our focus is sustainability, affordability, and future-ready technology—creating a world where every farmer grows more, earns more, and lives better.

Our Vision

Royalshetkari Information technology aims to become a leading global Agri-tech-Farm company that transforms farmers' lives through technology and AI, enabling sustainable growth, higher income, and a brighter future for farming communities.

Our Mission

The mission of Royalshetkari is to provide complete technological agricultural solutions—reducing farmers' production costs, improving market prices, and increasing their income. We empower farmers with affordable organic fertilizers, AI-driven tools, modern farming techniques, and sustainable support systems for long-term prosperity.
